MoveCrew Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 20, 2025

Introduction

MoveCrew ("we", "our", or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Information We Collect

1. Location Data (GPS)

  • What: GPS coordinates (latitude, longitude, altitude, accuracy)
  • When: Only during active workout sessions
  • Purpose: To display your hiking routes on maps and calculate workout statistics
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device and optionally synced to your Google account

2. Account Information (Optional)

  • What: Google account email address
  • When: Only when you choose to sign in with Google
  • Purpose: To enable cloud sync of your workout data across devices
  • Storage: Managed by Google Sign-In service

3. Device Information

  • What: Device model, operating system version
  • When: When using the app
  • Purpose: For app stability, crash reporting, and performance optimization
  • Storage: Anonymous analytics data

4. Workout Data

  • Start/end time of workouts
  • Distance, duration, speed metrics
  • Elevation gain/loss
  • GPS route paths

How We Use Your Information

  1. Core Functionality: Display routes, calculate statistics, provide real-time tracking
  2. Cloud Sync (Optional): Backup and sync across devices
  3. App Improvement: Fix bugs, improve performance (anonymous data)

Data Sharing and Third-Party Services

Purpose: Display workout routes

Purpose: Alternative map display

Purpose: Display advertisements

Data Storage and Security

  • Local Storage: All workout data encrypted using Hive database
  • Cloud Storage: Optional backup to your Google account via HTTPS
  • Data Retention: Until you delete workouts or uninstall the app

Your Rights and Choices

  • Access and Export: View and export workouts as GPX files
  • Delete Data: Delete individual or all workouts anytime
  • Control Cloud Sync: Sign out to disable cloud sync
  • Opt Out of Ads: Disable personalized ads in device settings

Children's Privacy

MoveCrew is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Location Permission

MoveCrew requires location permission to function:

  • Foreground Location: To track your workout route while the app is active
  • Background Location: To continue tracking when screen is locked (only during workouts)

Data Deletion Requests

  1. Local Data: Settings → Delete All Data
  2. Cloud Data: Sign out and delete from Google account
  3. Account Deletion: Contact help.movecrew@gmail.com
